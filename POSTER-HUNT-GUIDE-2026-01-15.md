## üîç **SEARCH RESOURCES:**

### **1. Google Images** (Easiest)
```
[Movie Name] [Year] [Actor] poster
```

**Tips:**
- Add "Telugu" or "Tamil" for clarity
- Try "movie poster" vs just "poster"
- Add "high quality" or "HD" for better images
- Use Tools ‚Üí Size ‚Üí Large

**Example:**
```
Chennakesava Reddy 2002 Balakrishna Telugu poster
```

---

### **2. IMDb** (Most Reliable)
**URL:** `https://www.imdb.com/find?q=[Movie Name] [Year]`

**Steps:**
1. Search movie name + year
2. Click on the movie
3. Go to "Photos" tab
4. Find official poster
5. Right-click ‚Üí Copy Image Address

**Best for:** Modern films (1990+)

---

### **3. Wikipedia** (Good for Classics)
**URL:** `https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/[Movie_Name]_(film)`

**Tips:**
- Try both English and native language Wikipedia
- For Tamil films: https://ta.wikipedia.org
- For Telugu films: https://te.wikipedia.org
- Look in info box on right side

**Best for:** Classic films, legendary actors

---

### **4. TMDB** (Modern Films)
**URL:** `https://www.themoviedb.org/search?query=[Movie Name]`

**Tips:**
- Very good for 2000+ films
- High-quality posters
- Multiple poster options
- Right-click on poster ‚Üí Copy Image Address

---

### **5. Tamil/Telugu Film Databases**

**Tamil Films:**
- **TamilCineStuff:** Search actor filmography
- **Cinesouth.com:** Tamil film archive
- **Behindwoods:** Tamil movie database

**Telugu Films:**
- **TeluguOne:** Telugu cinema archive
- **Greatandhra:** Movie database
- **Eenadu:** Film section

---

### **6. YouTube Thumbnails**
**Search:** `[Movie Name] [Year] Full Movie`

**Tips:**
- Many old films uploaded to YouTube
- Thumbnail often has poster
- Right-click thumbnail ‚Üí Copy Image Address
- Look for official channels
