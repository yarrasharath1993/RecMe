## üéØ Poster Quality Guidelines

### ‚úÖ High Quality (Preferred)
- Resolution: 500px+ width
- Clear, not pixelated
- Original poster design
- Good contrast and colors
- Text readable

### üü° Medium Quality (Acceptable)
- Resolution: 300-500px width
- Slightly pixelated but usable
- Fan-made recreations (if well done)
- DVD/VHS covers

### ‚ùå Low Quality (Avoid)
- Resolution: <300px width
- Very pixelated
- Screenshots from films
- Watermarked heavily
- Wrong movie entirely

## üöÄ Pro Tips for Speed

### 1. Use Browser Extensions
- **Image Search Options**: Right-click search by image
- **Multiple Tab Manager**: Open 10 movies at once
- **Auto-screenshot**: Capture poster URLs quickly

### 2. Keyboard Shortcuts
- `Ctrl+T` - New tab
- `Ctrl+W` - Close tab
- `Ctrl+F` - Find in page
- `Ctrl+Shift+T` - Reopen closed tab

### 3. Search Patterns That Work

**For Modern Films (2000+)**:
```
"[Movie Title]" Telugu poster HD
"[Movie Title]" movie poster [Year]
[Hero Name] [Movie Title] poster
```

**For Classic Films (Pre-2000)**:
```
vintage telugu poster [Movie Title]
telugu classic cinema [Movie Title]
[Movie Title] [Year] original poster
```

**For Obscure Films**:
```
[Director Name] filmography [Movie Title]
[Hero Name] movies [Year] poster
telugu film [Year] [partial title]
```

### 4. Batch by Actor/Director
If a director made 10 films in the list:
1. Find their filmography page
2. Many posters in one place
3. Research all 10 at once

**Example**: If A. Kodandarami Reddy has 15 films in the list, find his complete filmography page and grab all posters.
